Cultural activities

In the municipality of Dosrius-Canyamars (2Km.) you'll find:

  • Church of Sant Andreu del Far, late gothic style, houses the tombs of the counts of Bell-Lloc.
  • Sanctuary of Corridor, in Catalan Gothic style, dates from the sixteenth century and rises up to 632 m. high, on top of the mountain with the same name.
  • Gothic church of Sant Esteve in Canyamars.
  • Dosrius Castle, situated on a hilltop (246 m high) north of the town of Dosrius in the region of Barcelona Maresme; at present only its ruins are preserved.

The park Montnegre - El corredor, situated on the Serralada Litoral of Catalonia, between the districts of Maresme and Vallès Oriental, covers an area of approximately 15,000 ha.

The park, in the Serralada de Marina, forms the north-eastern limit of the green ring of natural parks that surround the Barcelona conurbation.

Its landscape consists of Mediterranean woodlands set in steep, hilly terrain where you can go hiking, cycling, etc.
