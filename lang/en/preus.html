Prices

* Prices are per night whole house (14 pax) VAT included. There are additional beds available.

* There are 2 cots available.

* Prices include sheets and towels. If the stay is more than a week, there is a change of sheets and towels each week included in the price.

  • Day on weekend (min 2 nights): 475€/night
  • Week in mid-season (min 6 nights): 275€/night
  • Week in hight-season (min 6 nights): 330€/night
  • New Year, Christmas, long weekends and special days: Contact
  • Additional person: 20€/night

* Hight-season: From July, 1 to September, 30

Optional services prices:

  • Towels change: 6€ set.
  • Sheets change single bed: 6€ set
  • Sheets change double bed: 9€ set
  • Cleaning service: 12 €/hour


General rules

  • At the time of booking, the name, telephone number and ID card number of the person making the booking will be informed
  • Payment can be made by bank transfer and/or cash.
  • The reservation will be valid at the time you make the deposit, which will be 25% of the amount of the contracted stay.
  • Payment of a deposit of € 300 may be required and will be returned in full the day after departure if there are no damages.
  • Cancellations: Up to 15 days before arrival, the return will be 100% of the payment made in advance, otherwise the entire amount paid will be lost.
  • Tourist tax: It is a tax of the Generalitat de Catalunya that applies to the price of stays in tourist establishments. (Article 6.2 of Decree 129/2012). - The rate to apply is € 0.45 per night + VAT per person over 16 years, maximum 7 nights. It is not included in the price.
  • In case of damage, the customer will be responsible for the cost of the damage caused.
  • Once the stay is finished, the house must be left in the same conditions in which it was found.
  • In the stays of a week or more, the time of entry to the house will be from 5:00 p.m. and the departure time before 11:00 a.m.
  • Smoking is not allowed inside the house.
  • It is not allowed to play loud music so that it can disturb the rest of the neighbors.

Safety rules

  • It is absolutely forbidden to fire off the appropriate areas (fireplace and barbecue)
  • Use of sauna is prohibited to minors under 15 years.
  • In the facilities of the swimming pool, children must be accompanied at all times by an adult.
  • In the property there are few facilities for breeding horses. Access is prohibited without permission or presence of owners.
  • In case of violation of safety rules, the customer will be responsible for the consequences.
